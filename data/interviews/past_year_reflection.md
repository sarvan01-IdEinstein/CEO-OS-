# The Past Year Reflection
*A guided self-interview.*

**Instructions**: Set a timer for 30 minutes. Turn off your phone. Close your email. Answer these questions with your raw, unedited voice. Do not write what you "should" feel. Write what is true.

---

### The Highlights
**1. If you had to describe this year in three words, what would they be?**
-
-
-

**2. What are the 3-5 specific moments you are most proud of? (These can be private wins that no one else saw).**
-

**3. What was the best money you spent this year?**
-

**4. When did you feel the most "in flow" or alive?**
-

---

### The Lowlights
**5. What drained you the most this year? (Specific people, tasks, or recurring obligations).**
-

**6. Where did you act out of fear or avoid a hard decision?**
-

**7. What is one mistake you made that you are actually largely responsible for?**
-

**8. If you had to relive this year ten times on a loop, would you be satisfied? Why or why not?**
-

---

### Key Lessons
**9. What is the single biggest lesson you learned about yourself?**
-

**10. What is a belief you held at the start of the year that you no longer hold?**
-

**11. What do you need to forgive yourself for?**
