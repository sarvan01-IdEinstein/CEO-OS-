# The Future Self Interview
*Conversation with the 10-year-older version of you.*

**Context**: Imagine you are sitting across the table from yourself, 10 years from today. You have achieved your North Star. You are calm, successful, and joyful.

---

**1. What does your average Tuesday look like in 10 years?**
-

**2. What is the "Current You" worrying about right now that the "Future You" laughs at?**
-

**3. What decision is the "Future You" thanking you for making today?**
-

**4. What relationship did you invest in that paid the biggest dividends?**
-

**5. How is your health different then?**
-

**6. If the Future You could give the Current You one single sentence of advice, what is it?**
